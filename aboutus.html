Our History

N Gupta, the company's founder, opened the first Wax In The Jar Store in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, in 2001. The Store established the future chain's pattern of featuring an elegant, sophisticated, and luxurious decorative candle range in 2020, Fortune magazine ranked The Wax The Jar at number 1 on their Fortune List of the Top 100 Companies to Work for.

The Store traces its humble beginnings to The Elegant One, a successful store started by our founder, Mr. Nitish Gupta, that featured for the first time on the India's best decorative candle store.

Corporate Leadership

Nitish Gupta Chief Executive Officer

With great foresight and intuition, Our CEO Nitish Gupta decided to open a store to showcase his ancestral art of hand made decorative candles. Somehow he just knew that people would appreciate the hard work put behind making a candle by hand with atmost care. It was 2000 and he opened the first Wax In The Jar store in Kanpur, UP. The store was an immediate success and today there are more than 100 Wax In The Jar Store around India that share commitment to quality, innovation and hard work.
